Location

Sibiu, Romania

Sibiu, known as the enduro capital of Romania, offers a unique mix of adventure, history, and breathtaking mountain landscapes. Hosting the famous Red Bull Romaniacs event, the city attracts enduro enthusiasts from all over the world. With access to a variety of diverse trails stretching through the Carpathian Mountains, from technical paths to forest roads, Sibiu is the perfect destination for adrenaline seekers. Additionally, its medieval center provides a vibrant historical atmosphere, combined with opportunities for fun and relaxation, making Sibiu the ultimate destination for enduro tours.
