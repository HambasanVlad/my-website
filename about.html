About Us

Vasile Hambasan - The Mastermind with Over a Decade of Expertise

With over 10 years of experience guiding enduro tours, Vasile Hambasan is the stone of our team. His journey began in 2005, and since then, he created unforgettable rides for enthusiasts from all over the world,including one of his tourists was Andreas Lettenbichler. Vasile has worked, the most time for KTM tours, organizing countless adventures and leaving riders thrilled and satisfied. Vasile's expertise extends far beyond guiding, as a competitor, Vasile is an exceptional rider with an impressive racing background. He has participated in the legendary Erzbergrodeo twice, secured multiple national results, and competed extensively during his youth. His unparalleled experience, skill, and passion make him a true guide in the enduro world.

Vlad Hambasan - The Rising Star

Following in his father's footsteps, Vlad Hambasan is a rising star in the enduro world. With 3 years of experience guiding tours for all levels of riders, Vlad is not only an excellent guide but also an accomplished athlete. Driven to excel at the highest level of the sport, Vlad has already achieved remarkable results in his racing career.
Achievements:

  • Champion of Romania in Hard Enduro
  • 5th place in the Silver Class at the 2024 Red Bull Romaniacs.
  • Vice-Champion of Romania in Hard Enduro 2024.
  • 7th place in the Bronze Class at the 2022 Red Bull Romaniacs.
  • Competitor at the prestigious GetzenRodeo in 2023.
  • Many 1st places in the national stage of hard enduro and endurocross

Under the guidance of his father, Vlad continues to grow both as a guide and as a professional athlete. For Vlad, his father is more than a coach - he's a mentor and a lifelong inspiration. You can check some video's with him on instagram: here

Fabian - The Ultimate Sports Masseur

To complete your enduro experience, we offer access to one of the best sports masseurs in the business - Fabian. As the principal masseur of the Red Bull Romaniacs World Championship, Fabian works with elite riders, including Graham Jarvis, Teodor Kabakchiev, Mario Roman, Sonny Goggia, Suff Sella, and many more any time when they come to train or race in Romania. Whether you need recovery after an intense ride or simply want to relax, Fabian's expertise ensures that you leave feeling refreshed and ready for your next adventure.
